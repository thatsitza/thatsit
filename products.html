ToolTracker - Tool Tracking, Stock Keeping & Reporting

  • ToolTracker has been specifically tailored with the mining industry in mind to control workshop tool store inventory.
  • Two of the primary functions are the issuing of tools to workshop staff and receiving or returning of the tools back into the store.
  • Employee access cards are used to identify the workshop member requesting tools as well as the storeman on duty that is issuing the tools. A till slip printer provides a slip for the workshop member.
  • The storemen have a facility to query a tool by it's tracking number this allows them to quickly locate the tool in question and to find out whether it is in the store at the moment or to whom it may have been issued.

Muster Station - Electronic Evacuation Assistance

  • Emergency Assembly point electronic roster to assist the fire marshal and / or safety offices ensure that all employees are accounted for and at the assembly points in the event of an emergency.
  • Any employees that have not clocked (badged) at the assembly point are flagged in red and their last know location is displayed to the officers including their employee number and contact details are also provided.
  • Information about employees not on site or have not used their access card are displayed in yellow while all other employees that have been accounted for are displayed in green.
  • The list of employee names is displayed with green names at the bottom of the list, yellow names in the middle and red being the most critical at the top of the list.
  • The Muster Station is connected to the existing access control system, this allows employees to "tick off on the list" either by presenting their access control card or by entering their employee number on the keypad at the assembly point as they arrive. 

Access Control - Single Door Standalone Proximity Card 

  • Very simple single door standalone access control system for a maglock and exit push button, compatible with the standard 26 bit format wiegand cards.
  • It has a limited offline card memory allowing you to use the system       without a computer connected.
  • Comes with a basic computer application used to add or remove cards from the system.
  • This software also lets you use the system in online mode that lets you use an unlimited number of cards because the controller will then validate the cards against the database in the software.
  • You can also use it in mixed online and offline modes as well.

FTS - Full Tracking Software

  • Web based control room dashboard created for the Omnicomm Africa fuel management system that displays vehicle or bulk tank fuel levels and other status information on a real time basis. Also providing various alerts and automatic repositioning of the tank information on the screen so that the most critical item is always at the top of the dashboard.
  • FTS builds on the iVigil dashboard with added google maps for live vehicle gps tracking and all of the other vehicle monitoring capabilities and features that one requires to be able to manage a fleet effectively.
  • Easily switch between the live and historic views in one click while displaying only the vehicles you have selected.
  • In the historic view vehicle track information is shown directly on the map between your selected time periods also showing markers that indicate starts and stops.
  • Check your vehicles fuel levels etc. at any point on it's track by clicking the place in question. Various graphs and reporting tools are also available.
